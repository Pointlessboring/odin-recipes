Summoning spell to call forth an spirit from the beyond!

“Witches were a bit like cats. They didn’t much like one another’s company, but they did like to know where all the other witches were, just in case they needed them.”



Ingredients

Gather the following items to power the incantation:
  • a pint of oil
  • grounded bones
  • freshly drawn blood
  • a silver chalice
  • a knife

The setting:

This ritual is to be performed on the night of the New Moon.

  • Go deep into a dark damp area
  • Carve a pentagram unto the earth
  • Draw a Cleanse the area with a purification ritual using sea salt.
  • Using a compass, establish the alignment of your surrounding.
  • Moving clockwise, cast a magic circle

    • Preparations:

  • Cleanse you chalice and fill it with oil
  • Dip your knife into the blood, let 10 drops fall into the oil
  • Splinkle a few pinches of powered bones into oil.
  • Clean you knife and use it to stir the chalice's content
  • Pour the content into the pentagram you carved into the earth:

    • The Incantation:

    Blood of the Mother from whom we are born,
    sharpen my focus and strengthen my will.

    Spirit of the land, I call upon you.
    Answer the call of thy supplicant.

    I pluck you from the beyond and call you forth into this realm
    Show yourself, come forward and serve me

    That is my will, this is my command!
